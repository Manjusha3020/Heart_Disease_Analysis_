PulseView

PulseView is an interactive visualization module in the Heart Disease Analysis Dashboard that provides a clear and comprehensive overview of a patient's cardiovascular health. It displays key health indicators such as heart rate, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, age, BMI, blood sugar, ECG results, and heart disease risk in an easy-to-understand format. By presenting patient data through charts, graphs, and summary metrics, PulseView helps doctors, healthcare professionals, and users quickly identify patterns, monitor health trends, and support early detection of potential heart-related conditions, enabling faster and more informed clinical decision-making.
